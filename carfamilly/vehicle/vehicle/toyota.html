About Toyota

Toyota (founded in 1937) is a global automotive leader, known for pioneering mass production techniques and innovations like the hybrid Prius. It is celebrated for its reliability, eco-friendly technologies, and safety, receiving numerous awards such as World Car of the Year and recognition for sustainability and advanced technologies.
