About Audi

Audi, founded in 1909 in Germany, is a prestigious automaker renowned for its luxury vehicles and advanced technology. Known for the iconic four-ring logo, Audi represents a commitment to innovation, sophistication, and high performance. Audi's lineup includes sedans, SUVs, and sports cars, with models like the A4, A6, and Q5 gaining popularity worldwide. Audi is a leader in electric mobility, with its e-tron series showcasing the brand's dedication to sustainable, future-focused engineering. Audi is celebrated for its cutting-edge Quattro all-wheel drive system, digital innovations, and a design philosophy that emphasizes quality and precision.
